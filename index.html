What I'll need from you.

Five quick things — most brands can pull this together in ten minutes. Nothing formal required.

✓

Logo or product assets

Your logo, product photos, or renders — whatever you have. Even phone photos work.

✓

A short brief

Two or three sentences: what your brand does, and who it's for.

✓

The vibe

Premium? Playful? Minimal? Bold? One word is enough — I'll build the world around it.

✓

If you have one, great. If not, no problem — I'll propose a palette that fits.

✓

Length & platform

Where the video will live: Instagram, your website, paid ads — so I cut to the right format.
